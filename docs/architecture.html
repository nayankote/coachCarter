synced — FIT uploaded, not yet analysed  ·  analyzing — extract metrics in progress  ·  awaiting_feedback — email sent, waiting for reply  ·  processing — on-reply webhook received, generating report  ·  complete — coaching report sent and stored
Stuck recovery: sync-garmin retries synced + analyzing after 10 min · Manual rescue: finalize-coaching.js
